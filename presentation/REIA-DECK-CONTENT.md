## SLIDE 8: Score Breakdown
**THE 10-POINT SCORING SYSTEM**

Every deal is scored on 4 pillars:

🟢 **Yield (40%)** — Cash-on-cash return potential
🔵 **Discount (30%)** — How far below market value
🟡 **Urgency (15%)** — Days on market, price cuts, seller motivation
🔴 **Risk (15%)** — Tax reset impact, maintenance red flags

A score of 7+ means it's worth a deep look.
A score of 5-6.9 means proceed with caution.
Below 5? Move on.
